What's the difference between bison and buffalo?

Bison can help restore native grasses and flowering plants that will provide a better habitat for grassland birds.

Where else do the buffalo roam?

In October, The Nature Conservancy brought 30 bison to Nachusa Grasslands to help with prairie restoration--the first conservation herd in Illinois.
